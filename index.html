About Thundle

Music game • band hub • roadmap • updates

Version 2.1

ThunderPunch! is a band built on energy, fun, weirdness, and songs that don’t take themselves too seriously. Thundle is built entirely around the band’s catalog, turning the music into a daily guessing game.

Every clip you hear in Thundle comes from a real ThunderPunch! song. The better you know the catalog, the stronger your streak will be.

Listen on Spotify

Thundle was created by Jacob Hatfield — musician, teacher, and developer — as a way to combine original music, games, and personality into one weird little daily ritual.

The goal is simple: make something fun, replayable, and unmistakably ThunderPunch!.

  • Press Play Clip to hear a short section of the song.
  • Guess the song title using the input box.
  • Each wrong guess gives you a longer clip.
  • Daily mode gives you a main song and, if you clear it, a bonus song.
  • Endless mode lets you chase high scores and hardcore achievements.

Achievements reward all kinds of progress: streaks, Endless runs, special moments, hidden secrets, and full-catalog mastery.

  • Common to Legendary rarities
  • Some achievements are hidden until you unlock them
  • Major progress achievements can be awarded retroactively based on your existing save data

You can play as a guest or create an account.

  • Guest: progress saves only in your current browser
  • Account: syncs stats, achievements, daily progress, and Endless data across devices
  • If cloud and local progress differ, Thundle tries to keep the newer save
  • ThunderCoins + shop
  • Unlockable themes
  • More achievements
  • Leaderboard ideas
  • More profile/account polish

Thundle stores gameplay progress locally in your browser and, if you create an account, in cloud save storage tied to your account.

Your account email is used only for authentication. Thundle does not sell your personal data.
